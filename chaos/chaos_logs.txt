===================================================================
2PC BLOCKING FAILURE MODE
===================================================================
When coordinator crashes after receiving YES votes from all shards 
but before writing the decision to its WAL, shards remain in 
"in-doubt" state. They cannot commit (don't know decision) and 
cannot abort (already promised to commit). They block until 
coordinator recovers. This is the classic 2PC limitation that 
more sophisticated protocols (Paxos Commit, 3PC) attempt to fix.
